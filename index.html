Woodensupcompany

This project is a simple homepage and a first embryo of a online shop for wooden SUP boards. The homepage is adaptive for different sceensizes

mterminal

My favourite project and a web app. The idea is to have it running on a tablet on the wall right inside the front door in my home. It will be used as a terminal with different kinds of uses. It presents different information for current day and week like date, time, time for sunrise and sunset, wheather right now and later the same day and week, themeday, what's for dinner, to-do list and calendar etc. It will also be a help to keep track of when our dog has been out for a walk and when he needs to get the next one.
